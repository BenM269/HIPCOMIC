What Makes a Good Negotiator? According to Voss
  • Listens more than they talk.
  • Controls their emotions.
  • Asks better questions instead of better arguments.
  • Makes the other side feel safe and respected.
  • Stays calm even in conflict.
  • Seeks understanding before agreement.
